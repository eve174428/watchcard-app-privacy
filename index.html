Information Collected by Third Parties (Advertising and Analytics)

While I do not personally collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII), I utilize third-party services for analytics and monetization (advertising). These services collect data governed by their own privacy policies.

A. Advertising and Monetization (AdMob)

This App uses **Google AdMob** for serving advertisements. AdMob's SDK is integrated into the App and collects various types of data to provide personalized and non-personalized ads, and for advertising measurement, and fraud detection.

Data collected by AdMob typically includes:

  • Identifiers: Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID, IDFA) used to link your device to ad campaigns.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the ads and the App.
  • Diagnostic Information: Performance and crash data related to the ads.

By using the App, you consent to the processing of data by Google AdMob in the manner and for the purposes set out in Google's Privacy Policy.

B.Translation Services

The App also uses the following services:

  • Translation Services: Lingva Translate (which acts as a privacy-friendly front-end for Google Translate) is used to process translation requests. The text you enter for translation is transmitted to this service, but I do not store this content.

The collection and processing of data by these services are governed by their respective privacy policies.
